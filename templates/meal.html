On Tuesday nights a regular group from nearby suburbs meet for a shared pre-prepared meal, friendship and conversation around a table in the Welcome Centre. For some attendees this is their only external interaction during the week, so it is a most important social occasion for them.
There were lots of friends from Open House and others in the community present, including a 30 year plus volunteer. During the seasons cricket or footie are often topics of discussion.
