Backyard Sports

When: Every Thursday 3:45pm - 5:15pm

Where: Malahang Reserve

Every Thursday after school Open House’s youth workers run a popular outdoor games session for 30 or more boys and girls in a local park – the Malahan Reserve. It’s all about having fun in a safe environment and everyone is welcome. Games include soccer, footie, basketball and more.

Our program provides youths with an opportunity to get out of the house, away from television and computer screens, and something healthy and fun to look forward to.

Some participants, once they experienced the friendly nature of our offering, later found their way to on site programs at our Macleod facilities.
