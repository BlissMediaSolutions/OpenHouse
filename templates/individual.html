Individual Support

The Open House team of staff and volunteers provide a wide range of personalised individual support offerings including:

  • Visits to participants at home, be that house, apartment or retirement home;

  • Visit to participants in hospital or other facility;

  • Accompaniment to appointments at the nurse, doctor, dentist, laboratory, or other medical professionals;

  • Accompaniment to appointments at Human Services, banks, other organisations; and

  • Prayers in time of need.
