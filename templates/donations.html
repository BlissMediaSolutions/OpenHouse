Support A Participant

Help turn around a young person’s life by sponsoring them on the Open House youth mentoring program for a year. We need your help because not all young people or their families are able to afford the fees of $120 per month plus trip costs (over 10 months).

To sponsor a teen for one year costs just $1,500 per annum. If the teen goes on to employment during the year we will use your funds for another needy person.

Bequests

We are grateful to those donors who have in the past remembered open house in their estate planning. Bequests to open house contribute significantly to our long term financial well-being and help ensure the success of our many programs on an ongoing basis.

Naming open house in your will is not difficult and you can designate any amount of your estate that you wish.

As you make plans for your estate we hope that you will remember a request to us.

For more information on bequests and other gifts to open house please contact our reception on 9450-7600
