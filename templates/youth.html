Y.W.M.P

Youth Workshop Mentoring Program

When: Every Tues, Wed, Thurs and Friday 9:00am - 3:00pm (During the School Semester)

Where: The Welcome Centre Workshop, Open House

This workshop program, which runs for school age boys and girls, equips them with skills and experiences which open up employment opportunities. It involves a great variety on hands on work in crafts, woodwork, mechanics, building, engineering and other areas, all supervised by skilled team members and volunteers. Many older children “graduate” form this program and go on to apprenticeships or fulltime employment.
