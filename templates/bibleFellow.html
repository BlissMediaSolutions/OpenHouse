Every Thursday an enthusiastic group met at Open House to praise God by way of singing, Bible study and fellowship. Each week a different guest leads the worship, so there was lots of variety.
A special feature of the Bible Fellowship is the presence and guidance of the Open House founders George and Muriel Farrington, and the chairman, David Woods.
For those who have difficulty with transport Open House offers a pick up and drop off in our new Renault bus.
“It is so spiritually uplifting to sing, pray and study the bible together each week,” said one of attendees.
