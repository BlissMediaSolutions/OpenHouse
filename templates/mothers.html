Play Group

When: Every Thursday between 10am - 12pm

Where: The Manse Centre, Indoor and Outdoor at Open House

Every Thursday between 10am and noon, Open House offers a free playgroup for babies, young children and their care givers. We have an indoor – outdoor facility with lots of toys and equipment that children love.

Most of all it’s a chance for mothers, fathers, grandparents, and other care gives to relax and have a friendly chat while their children play together.
